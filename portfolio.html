PROFESSIONAL PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT:

The project “Professional Portfolio Management” aim is to develop an android application to create and manage portfolios about a project in an organization. The portfolio creator can do the works like team members assigning, assign tasks, their roles and timeline to each member in the team. Once the project creation is over, team members can view their tasks and timeline for each task. They can choose if they completed the tasks before the deadline or not. They can also upload files and send it within the deadline of the project. Project Assigner gets notified on the status of each and every task. Once the portfolio of the project has been completed, the Assigner can archive the Portfolio. Email verification and OTP verification is also done in the application.

AN ALGORITHM FOR PREDICTING THRESHOLD ONLINE MARKETPLACE FOR TV ADS:

Marketing a product plays an important role in every business. Advertising through TV Channels is one of the best ways to reach the customers. But reaching out to a tv channel and advertising at a convenient price and time is quite hard to do. Managing multiple types of ads for every commercial break and pricing each ad are also hard for TV Channels. This problem can be solved with the help of Mobile Application called “Online Marketplace for TV Ads”. It’s a great innovative application that solves the problem of Advertising ads and to manage them. The TV Channel has to post their ad slots for different timelines at different prices. The Advertiser has to choose the ad slot and upload their ad video. The TV Channel will approve or decline the ad video. If the TV Channel has approved the ad video, the Advertiser can choose the number of ads and can book the ad slot. TV Channel can decline an ad video by sending a message about the reason for the rejection. TV Channel can post three different types of ads called “Normal, Featured and Auction ads” based on their choice.
